Mango Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the mangoes, yogurt, milk, honey, and ice cubes. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into glasses.
  3. Garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired and serve immediately.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • Fried onions and boiled eggs for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, chili powder, and biryani masala. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  6. Stir in the yogurt, cilantro, and mint. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Add the rice and 4 cups of water to the pot. Stir well and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat and let it sit for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. Garnish with fried onions and boiled eggs if desired and serve hot.
